SCTP HCM Organisation Learning and Development

This is an assignment submitted for a fictitious company, GreenTech Solutions Pte Ltd, for its customer support team's learning and development plan.

Executive Summary

The report outlines a comprehensive training development plan to address performance gaps within the Customer Service Team (CST). The goal is to enhance client engagement, product knowledge, and technical skills to improve customer satisfaction and increase revenue. Two key training programmes are proposed, with strategic use of government grants to manage costs and maximize ROI.

Purpose: To develop a training plan to enhance CST performance and address key business challenges.

Strategic Analysis & Performance Gaps

Performance Gap

Low customer satisfaction and recurring service escalations identified as key issues.

Business Impact

Decreased customer loyalty, negative brand perception, and reduced revenue.

Business Goal

Achieve a 25% increase in customer satisfaction scores by Q4 2025.

Customer Support Team Training Development Plan

Proposed Training Plan Overview

The plan is a 3-month comprehensive program focused on client engagement and technical skills.

Training aims to improve client engagement, product knowledge, and technical skills to address performance gaps.

Logistics and Administrative Plan

Locations

Training will be conducted off-site and in-house with convenience and minimal disruption in consideration.

Schedule

The training schedule will be coordinated to avoid clashes and maintain operational efficiency.

Trainers

Training will be delivered by qualified external vendors and a dedicated in-house training team.

Programme One: Communication Excellence for Client Engagement

This external off-site two-day workshop focuses on crucial soft skills to improve customer interactions and satisfaction. Qualified external vendor will be picked to conduct the training.

Building Rapport & Trust
Active Listening & Questioning
Conflict Resolution & De-escalation

Learning Objectives

  • To equip CST members with effective communication skills for building strong client relationships.
  • To enable CST members to actively listen to client needs and respond with empathy and clarity.
  • To train CST members to handle difficult situations and de-escalate conflicts professionally.

Learning Outcomes

  • CST members will be able to build rapport and trust with clients more effectively.
  • A noticeable increase in positive customer feedback and satisfaction scores.
  • Reduced number of escalated customer service issues.

Programme Two: Technical Upskilling - Product Mastery

This four half-day on-site workshop is designed to deepen product knowledge and technical expertise for more efficient problem-solving. It will be co-designed with, and facilitated by, subject matter experts from the Technical Team.

Core Product Deep Dive
Technical Problem-Solving
New Product Updates

Learning Objectives

  • To provide in-depth product knowledge, including features, configurations, and best practices.
  • To enhance technical problem-solving skills, allowing for faster and more accurate issue resolution.
  • To ensure CST members are up-to-date with new product updates and functionalities.

Learning Outcomes

  • CST members will be able to resolve client issues with greater efficiency.
  • A reduction in the number of technical service escalations.
  • Improved ability to communicate technical information clearly to clients.

Government Grants & Subsidies

Leveraging government grants is crucial for managing training costs. Here is a comparison of two key schemes that GreenTech Solutions can tap on.

SkillsFuture Enterprise Credit (SFEC)

Advantages

  • High Subsidy Rate & Broad Coverage
  • Flexible for various business initiatives

Disadvantages

  • One-off S$10,000 credit (not renewable)
  • Time-consuming application process

WSQ Course Subsidies

Advantages

  • Significant cost reduction (directly applied)
  • Nationally recognized qualifications
  • High-quality, standardized curriculum

Disadvantages

  • Less flexible than custom programs
  • Potential administrative burden
